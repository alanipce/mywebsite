CaseAide® Note

CaseAide ® Note is a user-defined mobile keyboard reducing the time spent documenting notes. Individuals can use the keyboard with agency smartphones and tablets or with personal devices (iOS and Android). Individual users can subscribe to an account independent of their agency or agencies can subscribe to administrative accounts on behalf of their workers.

The App

Features

Custom Keyboard

A keyboard extension making the CaseAide® Note functionality accessible to the user on all apps on their device.

Modern architecture

A RESTful API encapsulates the business logic of the system and provides the core services to the android, iOS, and web app.

Subscription Management

Full support for autorenewing subscriptions and payment plans for organizations, and iOS In-App-Purchase subscriptions for iOS users.

CaseAide® Scholar

CaseAide ® Scholar helps improve case management skills by educating and training professionals via simulation.
